Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I play Trivia Quest: Brain Challenge?
A: It's easy! Choose a category you're interested in, and we'll present you with challenging multiple-choice questions. Select the answer you think is correct, and see how many you can get right!

A: We offer a wide range of categories, including history, science, sports, entertainment, geography, and many more! There's something for everyone.

A: Yes, the game is free to download and play. You may have the option to make in-app purchases for additional features or content, but they are completely optional.
Q: Do I need an internet connection to play?
A: Yes, an internet connection is required to play Trivia Quest: Brain Challenge. This allows us to fetch new questions and keep your progress updated.
