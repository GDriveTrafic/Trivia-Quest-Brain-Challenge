Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start playing Trivia Quest?
To start playing Trivia Quest, simply download our app from the App Store or Google Play Store. Once installed, create an account or log in using your existing credentials. Choose your preferred categories and start answering questions to earn points and climb the leaderboard.
What types of questions can I expect in Trivia Quest?
Trivia Quest covers a wide range of categories, including history, science, literature, sports, entertainment, and more. Our questions are carefully curated to provide a challenging and engaging experience for players of all knowledge levels. Expect a mix of multiple-choice and true/false questions.
